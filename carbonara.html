Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Bacon
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Eggs

Steps:

  1. Boil the pasta with salt and oil
  2. In a frying pan, add the bacon with no oil
  3. Add the pasta into the frying pan, mixing with the bacon
  4. Use a spoon from the boiling water
  5. Add grated parmesan chesse
  6. Add a raw egg yolk and mix with the pasta
