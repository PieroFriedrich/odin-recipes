Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Basil leaves

Steps:

  1. Mix Flour with water and salt
  2. Boil the pasta
  3. Put in a container and spread tomato sauce
  4. Add cheese
  5. Repeat process for many layers
  6. Add Basil and spices, take to oven
  7. Leave for 45 minutes in 450F
